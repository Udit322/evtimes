Tata Curvv EV deliveries begin — 47,000 bookings pending

Deliveries are now underway for one of Tata's most anticipated EV launches, with a large booking backlog still in the queue.

EVTimes Desk•4 min read•Today

Tata has started deliveries of the Curvv EV, turning one of the year's biggest booking stories into a real volume test for the company. The pending order bank suggests demand remains strong even as competition rises.

What happens next will depend on how quickly Tata can convert bookings into customer handovers without compromising the ownership experience. Delivery pace often shapes early sentiment as much as the product itself.

For the broader market, the Curvv EV is another signal that Indian buyers are now responding to design-led electric products, not only value-led ones.
